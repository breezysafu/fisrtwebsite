== Description ==

Business Wp is a business corporate wordpress theme child theme of bizlite. Using Business Wp – WordPress Theme you can easily create a modern and very elegant website for any service providing business with real content. Easily edit the content and compose the page layouts. Homepage layouts and useful inner pages to give a corporate look and feel. The layout looks beautiful at any size, either a laptop screen, iPad, iPhone or even Android Mobile. Plus, It includes all documentation. Business Wp is the beautiful Business WordPress Theme for any businesses, freelancers, agencies, consulting. Everyone can find a suitable page for itself. It's Perfect for any kind of business websites food, agency, startup, restaurant, bitcoin, e-commerce, shopping, blog, fashion, spa, corporate etc. What is in the theme, theme is,  HTML5 & CSS3 Valid Code, Responsive & Retina Ready, High Speed & Clean code, Coded with SEO in Mind, 404 Page. Home Page With slider, Services, Blog, Callout,  Client logo Showcase etc. Business Wp is compatible with all kind of page builder plugins like  divi builder, king composer, elementor,   Gutenberg blocks, visual composer and so many.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
